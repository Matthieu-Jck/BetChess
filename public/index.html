How it works

Rules

Play normally

  1. Your turn: one standard legal chess move.
  2. Your bet: drag one of your opponent's pieces to the square you think it will play next.

If your bet is correct

  • One bonus move next turn.
  • You may play two legal moves in a row before betting again.
  • You cannot move the same piece twice during that bonus turn.

If the opponent has a bonus turn

  • Your prediction is counted as correct if it matches either of the two moves they make.
Standard chess rules apply. Check, checkmate, stalemate, repetition, and insufficient material all work normally.
