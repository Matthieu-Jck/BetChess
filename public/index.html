Welcome to BetChess.

A game of chess where each turn, you will bet on your opponent's next move.

Challenge someone by clicking in the top right corner.

Or, learn the (simple) rules by clicking next to it.

The rules are simple:

  • All moves must follow the standard chess rules. See: Rules of Chess.
  • Each turn, you make a move first and then place a bet on what you think your opponentâ€™s next move will be.
  • If your bet is correct, you earn an extra move for your own pieces on your next turn.
  • If your opponent plays two moves, your bet succeeds if it matches either of their moves.

When playing twice in a turn:

  • You cannot move the same piece twice.
  • Both moves needs to be legal from the start of your turn. This means you play your second move as if you hadn't played the first one yet.
