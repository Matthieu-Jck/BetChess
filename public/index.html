How it works

Rules

One turn, three steps

  1. Opponent's turn: wait for their move to land.
  2. Your move: play one legal chess move.
  3. Your bet: drag one of your opponent's pieces to the square you think it will play next.

If your bet is correct

  • Your next turn starts with a bonus move.
  • You may play two legal moves in a row before betting again.
  • You cannot move the same piece twice during that bonus turn.

If the opponent has a bonus turn

  • Your prediction is counted as correct if it matches either of the two moves they make.
  • Their second move must use a different piece from the first one.
Standard chess still decides the game. Check, checkmate, stalemate, repetition, and insufficient material all work normally.
