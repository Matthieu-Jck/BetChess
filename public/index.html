How it works

Rules

Every turn has two parts:

  • Make your legal chess move.
  • Then predict your opponent's next move by dragging one of their pieces.

If your prediction is correct:

  • You earn one extra move on your next turn.
  • Your next turn becomes two normal chess moves, played one after the other.

When your opponent has a bonus move:

  • They may make two moves before the turn passes back to you.
  • Your prediction succeeds if it matches either move they played.

Standard chess rules still apply for legal moves, check, checkmate, stalemate, and insufficient material.
