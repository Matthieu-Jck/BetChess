How to reach us

BetChess support is handled through the public project repository so bug reports, gameplay issues, and feature requests all land in one place.

Open a GitHub issue View the repository Back to BetChess

What to include in a report

  • Your username and what your opponent saw, if relevant.
  • The device you used, especially if the issue only happens on phone.
  • The exact phase of the turn where the bug happened.
  • A screenshot or short clip whenever the board or timers looked wrong.
